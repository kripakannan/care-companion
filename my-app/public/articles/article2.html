Caregiving & The Passions Project

Caregiving is an essential role that requires emotional, physical, and mental dedication. The Passions Project showcases the stories of caregivers who bring joy and purpose to aging adults.

Inspirational Caregiving Stories

  • How caregivers help seniors stay engaged with hobbies and interests.
  • Emotional resilience and self-care for caregivers.
  • Community support programs that assist caregivers.
